How I got into soccer

I first got into soccer because of my parents, who got me into the sport at the age of around 2 or 3. I had never really started watching every weekend until the past few years, although I had watched infrequently before. I had decided to take a break from the sport when I was 15 and I have debated returning ever since. I had always thought that it was fun and I only stopped playing because of health reasons, which I think should be resolved by now. After I took a break from soccer, I had begun getting really into watching the sport and gaining a deeper understanding of the game, along with watching weekly and participating in fantasy leagues.
